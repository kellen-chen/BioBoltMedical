BioBolt Surgical Stapler

Innovative fixation for all types of hernia meshes — both biologic and synthetic.

About BioBolt

Mesh repair is a common surgical procedure following hernia repair — with over 1.4 million US procedures annually. BioBolt offers a patented stapler system that works with all mesh types, ensuring secure fixation while minimizing infection risks.

Our Technology

Traditional synthetic meshes are not fully biocompatible and have higher infection rates. BioBolt's unique stapler allows surgeons to securely fix biosynthetic and biologic meshes that naturally integrate with the body, resulting in lower complications and improved patient outcomes.
