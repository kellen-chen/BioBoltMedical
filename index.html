The Company

A surgical device platform, built around safety.

Founded in Tucson, Arizona, BioBolt Medical develops tools that protect patients during the highest-risk moments of surgery. Needle and trocar insertion during minimally invasive procedures can cause unintended injuries to abdominal organs and vessels — a risk that rises with every millimeter of tissue between the surgeon and their target.

Our devices are designed to remove that uncertainty. We currently have two proprietary assets: BioPAD, an FDA-cleared device for controlled access that is ready for immediate commercialization, and the BioBolt Surgical Stapler, a next-generation fixation technology in development.

Our Assets

Two devices. One mission: safer surgery.

FDA 510(k) Cleared

Primary Asset

BioPAD — BioBolt Pneumoperitoneum Assist Device

A single-use device that lifts the abdominal wall to enable safe, controlled Veress needle and trocar insertion — turning a blind, high-risk step into a consistent, intuitive one.

  • FDA 510(k) cleared (K233020) — ready for immediate U.S. commercialization
  • Validated in a 30-patient clinical study vs. standard-of-care towel clamps: faster, safer, more consistent
  • Backed by surgeon endorsements from Duke, Stanford, Penn, and Memorial Sloan Kettering
  • Protected by two granted US patents (11,690,649 and 11,871,961) with international filings pending
  • Platform-ready — extensible to trocar insertion, abdominal adhesions, and flexible retractor applications

Secondary Asset · In Development

BioBolt Surgical Stapler

A next-generation fixation device designed to eliminate 30–60 minutes of manual suturing. Biologic mesh fixation in approximately five minutes, with equivalent pull-out strength.

  • Addresses the ~30% of abdominal surgeries at risk for hernia
  • Patented, pre-clinical validation underway
  • Not yet FDA cleared — development ongoing
